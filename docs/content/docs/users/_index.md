# User Guide

Welcome to HatShop! This guide will help you navigate the store and make purchases.

## Getting Started

### Browsing the Catalog

1. **Departments** - The main navigation shows product departments (e.g., "Regional", "Nature", "Seasonal")
2. **Categories** - Click a department to see its categories
3. **Products** - Click a category to browse products

### Viewing Products

Each product page shows:
- Product image and thumbnails
- Product name and description
- Price
- Add to cart button
- Customer reviews (if available)

## Shopping Cart

### Adding Items

Click the "Add to Cart" button on any product to add it to your cart.

### Managing Your Cart

- View your cart by clicking the cart icon
- Update quantities using the +/- buttons
- Remove items by clicking the remove button
- See the total price including any applicable taxes

### Checkout

1. Review your cart contents
2. Proceed to checkout
3. Enter shipping information
4. Choose payment method (PayPal or Credit Card)
5. Confirm your order

## Your Account

### Creating an Account

1. Click "Register" in the navigation
2. Enter your email and password
3. Fill in your profile details
4. Verify your email address

### Managing Your Profile

Once logged in, you can:
- Update your shipping address
- View order history
- Track current orders
- Manage payment methods

## Payment Options

### PayPal

1. Select PayPal at checkout
2. You'll be redirected to PayPal
3. Log in and confirm payment
4. Return to HatShop for confirmation

### Credit Card

1. Select Credit Card at checkout
2. Enter card details securely
3. Submit payment
4. Receive confirmation

## Order Tracking

After placing an order:
1. Go to "My Orders" in your account
2. Click on an order to see details
3. View current status and shipping info

## Getting Help

If you encounter any issues:
- Contact support via the Help page
- Report bugs on [GitHub](https://github.com/wilsonify/hatshop/issues)
